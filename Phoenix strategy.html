Objective
Drive B2B and wholesale sales of DTF transfers and custom stickers into Ahwatukee and Chandler — two adjacent high-income Phoenix metro markets with strong small business, sports leagues, schools, and community event activity. This is a living strategy doc — everyone on the team is encouraged to challenge it, add to it, and push it further.

Open Questions
QuestionWhy It Matters
Is Ahwatukee/Chandler actually the right first move, or just the easiest data to pull?We picked these two because of available demographic data — worth confirming they beat alternatives like Gilbert or Tempe on actual fit, not just convenience.
Do we have the production capacity to fulfill a wholesale surge if 3+ screen printers say yes at once?Landing the wholesale accounts is the goal — but a capacity gap could turn a win into a reputation problem.
Is cold outreach the best entry point, or should Chamber membership come first everywhere?Chamber-first worked for Ahwatukee logic — does the same apply to Chandler, or does it slow us down there?
Alternative Ideas
IdeaRationale
