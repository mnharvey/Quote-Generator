Attention Vendors & Shop Owners
We're not just selling stickers — we're your production partner. Walk up to the booth and let's talk.
In Partnership with Veterans in Need Project
You're watching us press shirts live right now. That's DTF — Direct-to-Film transfer printing. It's what's on those shirts. We produce these in-house at our Maricopa facility and wholesale to shops, decorators, and vendors across Arizona. If you're buying transfers from somewhere else, we'd like to earn your business.
  • DTF transfers by the foot — gang sheets, individual transfers, wholesale pricing
  • Die-cut vinyl stickers — any design, any shape, weather-resistant
  • UV printing on hard goods — tumblers, keychains, acrylic, phone cases
  • Laser engraving — glass, metal, acrylic, wood, trophies, awards
  • Banners, foam board signs, corrugated yard signs, canopy tents
  • 48–72 hour standard turnaround from our Maricopa facility
  • No minimum order — single items to large production runs
  • SDVOSB certified — qualifies for government set-aside purchasing
