Promotional Products Guide
Not sure where to start? Every tier gets your brand in front of people — the difference is impression, durability, and perceived value. Choose by budget, audience, or occasion. We'll help you find the right fit.
How it works
Tell us your product category, quantity, budget, and timeline — we'll find the best options across all three tiers and present them side by side. Pricing varies by item and quantity. All quotes are built on the spot.
Tier One
Good
Gets your brand out there. Budget-friendly, fast, and functional.
Ask for quote →
  • Pens & Writing
    Ballpoint, stylus combo, retractable. High volume, low cost per unit.
  • Koozies & Cozies
    Foam or neoprene. Events, giveaways, sponsor tables.
  • Basic Tote Bags
    Non-woven or cotton. Trade shows, community events, handouts.
  • Keychains
    Metal or acrylic, custom shape or standard. Small, memorable, keeps moving.
  • Lanyards
    Breakaway or standard, full-color imprint. Schools, events, staff ID.
  • Buttons & Magnets
    Pinback buttons or fridge magnets. Low minimums, fast production.
Best for
Mass giveaways, community events, trade show tables, first-time buyers testing the water.
Tier Two
Better Most Popular
Higher quality, longer lifespan. Items people actually keep and use.
Ask for quote →
  • Drinkware
    Plastic tumblers, ceramic mugs, travel cups. Daily-use visibility.
  • Branded Apparel
    T-shirts, polos, hats. DTF or embroidery. Wearable advertising.
  • Journals & Notebooks
    Soft or hardcover, custom cover. Corporate meetings, onboarding kits.
  • Branded Bags
    Backpacks, drawstring, laptop sleeves. Higher perceived value than basic totes.
  • Umbrellas
    Auto-open, vented canopy, full-color. Weather it out branded.
  • Power Banks
    Pocket-size, laser-engraved or printed. Tech-forward, high retention.
Best for
Client appreciation, staff gifts, event sponsors, repeat customers, real estate closing gifts.
Tier Three
Best
Premium, gift-worthy, lasting impression. They'll remember where it came from.
Ask for quote →
  • Engraved Tumblers
    Stainless steel, laser-engraved. The gift people show off. Done in-house.
  • Premium Apparel Kits
    Embroidered jacket or vest, hat, and tumbler. Full branded kit in a box.
  • Leather Goods
    Portfolios, cardholders, padfolios. Debossed logo. Executive presence.
  • Tech Bundles
    Wireless charger, earbuds, power bank — co-branded, gift-boxed.
  • Award & Recognition Sets
    Engraved acrylic, crystal, or metal plaques. Employee of the year, milestones.
  • Custom Gift Boxes
    Curated multi-item kits, custom tissue and ribbon, branded box. Turnkey gifting.
Best for
Executive gifts, corporate onboarding, VIP clients, government recognition, high-value prospects.
